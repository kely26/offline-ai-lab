## How To Publish On LinkedIn

1. Open LinkedIn and click **Start a post**.
2. Choose **Add media** and upload the images in the order listed above.
3. Paste the **Main LinkedIn Post** text.
4. Make sure the line **built in Tanzania** remains in the post. This gives the story a strong personal and professional identity.
5. After publishing, add the **First Comment** immediately.
6. Reply quickly to early comments. Fast engagement helps the post travel further.
7. Send the post link to friends, colleagues, and communities that may react within the first hour.

## Best Visual Strategy

For the strongest first impression:

1. Use the hero image first.
2. Use the intro and feature slides next.
3. Keep the Tanzania story slide near the end.
4. End with the contact card so viewers know what to do next.

## Important Tone Guidance

Keep the post:

- professional
- confident
- product-focused
- privacy-aware
- not exaggerated

Avoid saying the product is the best in the world.
Focus on what it actually does and why it matters.
